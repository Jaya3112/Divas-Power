Hand Gestures
This feature can recognize human gestures
and provides a new way for people with
physical disabilities to interact with
computers.This will allow our users to
choose to interact with gesture-controlled
technology based on their individual needs

Eye Movement
We found it to be a novel idea to control
computer mouse cursor using human eyes movement.
This feature controls mouse moving by automatically
affecting the position where eyesight focused.So
users can now control the computer without worrying
about their body condition.

Speech To Text
This is a feature that converts words that
are spoken in real time or audio files to
electronic written text to support the users
with hearing aids and increased demonstration
of learning and independence.

Text To Speech
This feature can help people with vision
impairment and learning disabilities to listen
to written words from e-books, news articles
and many such sources,thereby enhancing the
overall learning and interaction experience.
